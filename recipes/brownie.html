Brownie

Description

This is a Brownie recipe from Preppy Kitchen The BEST Fudgy Brownie Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of Unsalted Butter (226g)
  • 3/4 cup of Coco Powder (75g) Dutch Coco Powder is recommended
  • 2 cups of Granulated Sugar (400g)
  • (optional) 1 teaspoon of Instant Espresso
  • 3 Eggs
  • 1 teaspoon of Vanilla Extract (15ml)
  • 1/4 teaspoon of Sea Salt
  • 1 1/2 cup of semisweet Chocolate (270g)
  • 1 cup of All-Propose Flour (120g)

Steps

  1. Melt the butter
  2. Put Coco Powder and Granulated Sugar to the melted butter, use sifter to make them fine
  3. Add Instant Espresso to amplify the chocolate flavor
  4. Mix the ingredients until sugar is all dissolved
  5. Add Eggs, Vanilla Extract, and Sea Salt to the mixture
  6. Mix well
  7. Chop the Chocolate into pieces
  8. Add half of the Chocolate and Flour to the mixture
  9. Mix it gently
  10. Prepare the baking mold, put a cooking paper inside
  11. Put the mixture to the mold, top it with the other half of Chocolate
  12. Bake it in the oven, 350 F for 35-40 minutes (depend on your preference of texture)
